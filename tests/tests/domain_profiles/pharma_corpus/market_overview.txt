The global pharmaceutical market reached approximately $1.48 trillion in 2024. Growth is driven by biologics, oncology therapies, and GLP-1 receptor agonists used in metabolic disease and weight management. Analysts forecast the market to reach $1.9 trillion by 2028 at a CAGR of around 6.5 percent. The GLP-1 segment alone is projected to roughly double over the same horizon, with Eli Lilly and Novo Nordisk holding a combined ~92% share. Biosimilar penetration is expected to climb to about 35% of biologic revenue by 2027.

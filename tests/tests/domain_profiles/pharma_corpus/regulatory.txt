The FDA approved 55 novel drugs in 2024, with continued emphasis on accelerated approval and rare-disease designations. EMA's rolling review process has shortened the time-to-decision for select rare-disease therapies. Real-world evidence (RWE) is increasingly accepted by both agencies for label expansions and post-approval commitments. The Inflation Reduction Act has introduced Medicare price negotiation for selected high-spend drugs, with the first negotiated prices taking effect in 2026.

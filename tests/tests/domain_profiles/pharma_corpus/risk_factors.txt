Risk factors for pharmaceutical issuers in the 2024-2028 window are dominated by four themes. Patent cliff exposure: more than $200 billion of annualized revenue is associated with branded products losing exclusivity through 2028. IRA pricing negotiations: Medicare price-setting under the Inflation Reduction Act affects 20+ blockbusters, compressing margins. Supply chain concentration: active pharmaceutical ingredient (API) production remains heavily concentrated in China and India, exposing the industry to geopolitical and logistical shocks. Regulatory tightening: FDA and EMA have raised expectations for post-market surveillance and real-world evidence, increasing lifecycle compliance costs.
